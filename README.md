# airbnb-analytics-munich
Data-driven revenue analysis and market segmentation of Munich Airbnb listings using OLS regression and K-Means clustering. Developed at Seoul National University (SNU).

**Project Overview**

Data-driven revenue analysis and market segmentation of Munich Airbnb listings.

Developed at Seoul National University (SNU) Business School.

Course: "Introduction to Business Analytics".

The data was provided by "Inside Airbnb".

This project was given 23.7 out of 25 points.

**Collaboration & Tools**

Group Project: Collaborative effort at SNU.

LLM Usage: Use of Large Language Models was allowed and encouraged for code and documentation.

**Methodology & Focus**

Objective: Focused on identifying correlations, not causal relationships.

Model Note: Multicollinearity was intentionally not addressed as the priority was pattern recognition and predictive potential.

Tech Stack: Python (Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Statsmodels, Seaborn).

**Analytical Steps**

EDA: Data cleaning and creation of a "Potential" metric (Predicted vs. Actual Revenue).

OLS Regression: Estimating annual revenue with feature selection based on p-values and K-Fold Cross-Validation.

K-Means Clustering: Segmenting the market to find undervalued listings and investment opportunities.
